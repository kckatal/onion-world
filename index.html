Why Onions?

Onions are the soul of the kitchen — humble yet bold, tear-inducing but worth every sting. From the sweet whisper of shallots to the fiery declaration of red onions, they flavor our food, our stories, and our tears.

Whether roasted, raw, or caramelized into oblivion, onions are life layered in papery skins.
